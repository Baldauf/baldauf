Open Pixel

The best open source stock photography. FInally searchable in one place.

Unsplash. SplitShire. Magdeleine. There are so many incredible stock photography sites out there, and quite a few of them are even open source. I’ve found, however, there are no good tools or methods to look across these various sources and find the perfect, free, and tasteful photo when you know exactly what you’re looking for.

My role: As a co-founder and digital lead, I have owned everything in this entrepreneurial effort from the user model to the AWS configuration. I certainly prefer the former to the latter, but this has been a cool project and incredible ongoing learning experience that I'm excited to continue iterating on.
