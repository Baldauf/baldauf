Pandora Radio

One awesome brand stands in the sea of many.

Pandora Radio approached my Fireant Team to create an iPad app to showcase their vision of the future and new product features at the 2014 Consumer Electronics Show.

My role: I worked with one of our designers to develop a rich and engaging custom illustrated UI. I then crafted unique interface elements that were simple and unassuming enough to let the illustration shine, yet strong enough to still prioritize our message.
